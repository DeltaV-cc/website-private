Privacy Policy

1. Our Commitment

Delta V SRL ("Delta V", "we", "us", or "our") goes beyond legal minimums to protect your privacy. While compliant with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Romanian Law No. 506/2004, we adopt stricter standards to ensure your data is handled with care, transparency, and respect. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data when you use deltav.cc (the "Site") or our services.

2. Data Controller

Delta V SRL, registered with the Trade Registry under number [51301377], located at [Bucharest], Romania, is the data controller. Contact: engage@deltav.cc or Signal @DeltaV.01.

3. Data We Collect

We collect only what’s strictly necessary:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, phone number (if provided), and message content submitted via our contact form or direct communication. Form data is collected by Delta V, forwarded via Cloudflare Email Routing, and stored in a Gmail account managed by Google. Cloudflare and Google may process this data, potentially outside the EU, under their respective terms.
  • Usage Data: Minimal analytics (e.g., anonymized IP address, page visits) via privacy-first tools; no invasive tracking.
  • Third-Party Data: Information from secure channels (e.g., Signal) if you contact us there.

We do not collect sensitive data (e.g., biometric, health) unless explicitly required for a service and consented to by you.

4. How We Use Your Data

We process data solely for:

  • Responding to your inquiries and delivering Services (e.g., IT, AI, crypto consulting).
  • Improving the Site with anonymized insights.
  • Legal compliance (e.g., tax obligations).

Legal bases include explicit consent (opt-in via form submission), contract necessity, and limited legitimate interests (e.g., security). We avoid broad or vague processing purposes.

5. Cookies

We use only essential cookies for Site functionality (e.g., session management). No advertising or third-party tracking cookies are deployed. You can disable cookies via browser settings without losing core functionality.

6. Data Sharing

We share data only when unavoidable:

  • With Cloudflare (Email Routing) and Google (Gmail) to process and store contact form submissions. Cloudflare operates under its Data Processing Addendum, while Gmail operates under Google’s Terms of Service. Both may store data outside the EU.
  • With authorities, only under a verified legal mandate.

We never sell, rent, or monetize your data. Third-party recipients are contractually bound to process data securely, though Gmail’s consumer service lacks a formal GDPR Data Processing Agreement.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

We delete your data faster than required by law:

  • Contact Data: Personal data from inquiries (e.g., name, email, message) is deleted from Gmail within 90 days of resolving your request, unless you request otherwise or a contract requires retention.
  • Client Records: Data tied to Services (e.g., contracts, invoices) is retained for 1 year after the service ends, unless tax or legal obligations mandate longer (max 5 years under Romanian law), then deleted promptly.
  • Usage Data: Anonymized analytics are kept for 30 days, then purged.

You can request immediate deletion at any time (see Section 8), and we’ll comply unless legally prevented. We proactively notify you before deletion deadlines to confirm your preferences.

8. Your Enhanced Rights

Beyond GDPR, we offer:

  • Access: Full data reports in human-readable format within 15 days (faster than GDPR’s 30 days).
  • Rectification/Erasure: Immediate updates or deletion from Gmail upon request, no questions asked, unless legally required to retain.
  • Portability: Data in open formats (e.g., CSV, ODT).
  • Proactive Disclosure: We’ll notify you of any data use changes before they occur.
  • Complaints: File with us directly

Exercise rights at engage@deltav.cc or Signal @DeltaV.01.

9. Security

We exceed GDPR with:

  • End-to-end encryption for all communications (e.g., Signal integration).
  • Secure forwarding via Cloudflare Email Routing (TLS) and storage in Gmail (encrypted at rest).
  • Regular third-party security audits

Despite this, no system is infallible; use at your own risk.

10. International Transfers

Data is processed in the EEA by Delta V. However, Cloudflare and Google (Gmail) may transfer data to the US or other regions under their terms. We rely on Cloudflare’s Data Processing Addendum for safeguards, but Gmail’s consumer service does not offer Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs). We mitigate risks with encryption and transparency.

11. Third-Party Links

Links to third-party sites (e.g., Signal, Cloudflare, Google) are not our responsibility. We vet them for privacy but can’t control their practices.

12. Updates

We’ll notify you of changes at our discretion. Continued use post-update implies acceptance.

13. Contact Us

For privacy concerns, reach us at engage@deltav.cc or Signal @DeltaV.01.
