# /goal — Delta V Website v2: Sovereign AI Engineering Forge

**Owner:** default (coordination) + chiefcreative (positioning) + chiefstaff (business architecture)

## Exact Outcome
Rebuild the public face of Delta V at deltav.cc as a high-signal, business-clear **Sovereign AI Engineering Forge**.

The site must make it immediately obvious what Delta V actually sells, how the offerings fit together as a system, and why we are meaningfully different from generic Web3 agencies or opsec consultancies. It must close the "AI Engineer gap" by clearly signaling deep, production-grade, sovereign AI engineering capability.

## Context & Honest Critique of Current State
The existing deltav.cc reads as a competent but generic services company. It lacks:
- Clear product architecture (flat list of services instead of a coherent system)
- Strong AI engineering signal
- Sovereign / Zero Headcount Company identity
- Business-level clarity for sophisticated clients

We want to evolve from "decentralized technology services" to "Sovereign AI Engineering Forge" while keeping the professional, numbered, high-end aesthetic of the current deltav.cc.

1. **Agent Forge** — Custom sovereign AI agents & tooling (core AI engineering product)
2. **OpSec Retainer** — Ongoing visual + operational security system
3. **Zero-Trust Training** — Structured capability building for teams and executives

IP Studio remains internal only.

- Keep the same visual language and beauty level achieved in the current build (numbered sections, strong typography, premium cards, dark matte aesthetic)
- Static export + Cloudflare Pages
- No heavy client-side libraries
- Use existing Next.js + Tailwind stack
- All content must be high-signal and business-understandable

## Deliverable
- Updated homepage with clear product architecture and strong AI engineering positioning
- Refined offerings section that shows how the three systems work individually and together
- Improved hero and value proposition that closes the AI engineer gap
- Maintain all existing security hardening (`_headers`, SRI, CSP)

- Business clarity (a sophisticated client can immediately understand what we sell and how it works)
